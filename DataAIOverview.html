DataAI Overview

It's time to put the Internet and AI to work to increase mobility and to make the creation and processing of reports convenient, simple, and accessible for end users and administrators alike.

What Does DataAI Do?

Given a connection string, DataAI automatically analyzes the structure of your database and creates reports, charts, maps, and statistics which you can customize or use directly. It analyzes data using Data Analytical and Artificial Intelligence.

The idea is to create a universal analytical and reporting system independent from the database that end users can easily operate without any programming or database knowledge.

As soon as you register and establish a connection to your database, DataAI analyzes the structure of the database and generates a variety of tabular, graphical, and statistical reports, giving you a powerful and convenient interface for data exploration and analysis.

DataAI enables end users to create their own customized reports and analytics transparently, based on data in existing databases hosted by your organization. It makes data analysis and report creation a fast and simple task that can be done by technical personnel after only a few hours of training.
